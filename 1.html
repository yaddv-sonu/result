    Your Answer: a wealthy plantation owner
    Correct Answer: a character from a racist minstrel show

  7. Which important function did the 1961 Freedom Rides serve?
    Your Answer: They challenged racially segregated bus facilities.
    In 1946 the U.S. Supreme Court had deemed segregation aboard interstate buses illegal, and in 1960 that ruling was extended to bus station facilities, such as restrooms. In 1961 a group of seven African Americans and six whites shared a bus on a trip through the South to put the extended ruling to the test. They were beaten, and their bus was firebombed.
